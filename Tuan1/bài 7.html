A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth.It is
belived it take about 80 muscles to smile.Some smiles include a contraction of the muscle at the corner of the eyes,
an action known as a "Ducheme smile".Smile performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
